Translations
============

At the moment ATC-NG only supports English (oh, well, at least the English the
developers speak!).

There are no immediate plans to make it a multi-language
application, but this might change should the demand arise in the future.

Should you decide to **translate part or all of the game documentation**, please
let us know: it would be nice to coordinate, or at least put a link to your work
from here!
